Our Partners

Pragmatec

Pragmatec was founded in Vienna in 2013 as a technology company, in order to develop and market practical and innovative technical solutions, mainly those resulting from its own research. A special focus was placed on alternative energy and environmental technology.

Another goal was to build well-chosen partnerships based on a network of relevant areas, established over decades, as well as partnerships with share participation with interesting and clear potential from a strategic, business and commercial perspective.


Green Environment

We have a partnership wit Green Environment and they offer all the services that are related to Oil and Gas. They also do projects that consists of constructing, installing reporting, monitoring and on ongoing maintenance of Oil and gas sectors. Correspondingly, they offer environmental assessment, studies and compliance to sustain transmissions projects. Moreover, they support the revival of the industry.


Sorema

Sorema: We have a joint operating partnership with Sorema one of the leading recycling equipment manufacturers with solid and proven performance in similar project partnership globally and manufacturing experience of over 7 decades as well as recycling equipment manufacturing of over 4 decades.
